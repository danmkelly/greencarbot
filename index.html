About EcoDrive Advisor

Data source: EPA Fuel Economy Guide MY2025 – 1,410 passenger vehicles (ICE, EV, PHEV).

Rankings: Vehicles are ranked by tailpipe CO₂ emissions (g/km). For EVs, this is zero at tailpipe. Consider your local electricity grid mix when evaluating total lifecycle emissions.

Limitations: This tool provides environmental comparisons based on EPA data. It does not account for manufacturing emissions, battery production impacts, or end-of-life disposal. Always verify with current EPA ratings.

Privacy: Conversations are stored only in your browser (localStorage). Nothing is sent to any server except for AI processing (optional).
