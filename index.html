About GreenCarBot

Data source: EPA Fuel Economy Guide MY2025 (1,410 passenger vehicles).

What this does: Estimates your personalised real-world CO₂ emissions by combining EPA vehicle data with your driving behaviour, charging habits, and electricity source.

What this does NOT do: Full lifecycle analysis, manufacturing emissions (except battery embedded CO₂ amortisation), or total cost of ownership.

Important: EPA laboratory values do not represent real-world driving. Real‑world fuel consumption is typically 10–20% higher. PHEVs often underperform their EPA ratings when not charged regularly.
