💾 What Beep-Beep stores

Beep-Beep remembers a few things to make your car search better -- all stored on your device, never sent to a server:

  • Your name (if you tell him)
  • Cars and makes you've searched for
  • How many times you've visited
  • Your last visit date
  • Your current search filters

This lets Beep-Beep greet you by name, recall past searches, and pick up where you left off. To remove everything, click Clear memory beside Beep-Beep's status badge.
